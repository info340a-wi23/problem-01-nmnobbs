Appa

Appa is an flying air bison from the animated television series Avatar: The Last Airbender. Appa is a guide and friend of the main character of the show, Aang. The flying bison has a brown arrow on their forehead that stretches along the back.

Reasons I Admire Appa

  • Appa is incredibly loyal and a reliable animal.
  • Appa can fly and adds a lot of quality content to the show.
  • Appa is just so cute and adorable.
